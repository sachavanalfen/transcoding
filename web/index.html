During The eight year long Iran - Iraq war, the Islamic Republic of Iran published a series of postal stamps related to the war, and used them to show the logic of the war and to attract public support. This website shows the collection of Iran - Iraq war stamps which contains of 114 pieces. The stamps were printed and published by "the organization for preserving and publishing of relics and values of the Holy Defense Army" inform. >
